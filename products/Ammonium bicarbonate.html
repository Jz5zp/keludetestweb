    Chemical formula: (NH4)HCO3
Product Description

CAmmonium bicarbonate is an inorganic compound with formula (NH4)HCO3. The compound has many names, reflecting its long history. Chemically speaking, it is the bicarbonate salt of the ammonium ion. It is a colorless solid that degrades readily to carbon dioxide, water and ammonia.

Product Specification
Usage

1. Used as a nitrogen fertilizer for a variety of soils, providing both ammonium nitrogen and carbon dioxide for crop growth.

2. Used as analytical reagent, also used for synthesis of ammonium salts and fabric degreasing.

3. Can promote crop growth and photosynthesis, promote seedlings to grow leaves, can be used as a chasing fertilizer, also can be applied directly as a base fertilizer, used as a food swelling agent.

4. Used as advanced fermenting agent for food. Combined with sodium bicarbonate, it can be used as the raw material of bread, cookies, pancakes and other bulking agents, and also used as the raw material of foaming powder juice. Also used for scalding and bleaching of green vegetables, bamboo shoots, etc., as well as medicine and reagents.

5. Used as buffering agent; inflating agent.

6. Ammonium bicarbonate is widely used in plastic and rubber industries, pottery making, chrome tanning leather and synthetic catalysts.

7. Used for precipitation of separated compounds in light rare earths
