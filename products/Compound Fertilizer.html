Product Description

Compound fertilizer is a fertilizer containing two or more nutrients in nitrogen, phosphorus and potassium. Compound fertilizer has the advantages of high nutrient content, few by-products and good physical properties, etc. It plays a very important role in balanced fertilization, improving fertilizer utilization and promoting high and stable crop yield.
