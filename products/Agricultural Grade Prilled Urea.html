Product Description

Urea fertilizers are widely used in agriculture. They are considered an economic nitrogen source. Urea fertilizers are highly soluble (solubility of 1079 g/L at 20ºC). Therefore, in addition to soil applications, urea fertilizers can be also used in fertigation or as a foliar application. Besides, urea can also be used to process compound fertilizers
