About Us

Company Profile

Kelude Agricultural Group was established in 2019. We specialize in fertilizer products such as urea, ammonium sulfate, compound fertilizer and feed additives. We also supply and sell a wide range of chemical products such as melamine and sodium bicarbonate. Urea 46% Prilled and Urea 46% Granular is our key products. While supplying industrial urea, automotive urea and agricultural urea is our core business, we have been developing other chemical products for agricultural and industrial use. Currently, we have annual sales of 300,000 tons of various products. Our markets cover China, USA, Canada, EU, Africa, Australia, Japan, Korea, Indonesia, Malaysia, Israel, Vietnam, Myanmar, India, Pakistan, Brazil, Argentina and other countries and regions.
